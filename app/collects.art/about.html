Collects is an anonymized social network in the style of the artist Bunny Rogers. Create your own collects or view the work of others at collectable.art.

Collects

Select your adoptable ribbon in the lower left
Create a collect using the button in the lower right

When you swipe on a collect you can:

  • Make it public on collectable.art (private collects are always available via the button in the collect view)
  • Close it to further additions if you are done collecting
  • Delete it

Collect

View your collect in a browser
Rename your collect
Select a template to format your collect
Create an entry in your collect

Swipe on an entry to delete.

Entry

Each entry has an image and a text field. Use the back button to save your progress.
